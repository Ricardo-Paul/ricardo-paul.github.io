Valentine's Day (fet lanmou ❤️), my thoughts.

Ricardo Paul, written on
2021-02-14
thoughts

disclaimer: I am by no means a relationship expert so take everything I have to say in some regards with a grain of salt.

Today is Valentine’s day, it is a reminder for us to celebrate our loved ones. If you are in love now, you probably are hyped up about today, thinking about giving or receiving some chocolate, I should say if you’re lucky to receive, it depends. But the idea is today is a meaningful day to you.

On the other hand, if you’re not in love, today is just another normal day, a Sunday like every Sunday, maybe you’ll go to church and back being stuck at home going over your normal activities.
Regardless of which category you fall in at the time of reading this, you may find these few insights useful.

In our life, we go through beautiful positive experiences as well as some we tend to deem negative, whatever you think they are, we always come stronger and a better person out of them, they are those that strengthen us the most and build our character.

As I opened quora this morning, I read through many great answers to meaningful questions, I think I’ll just share a couple of ones related to relationship with you.

1 - It’s better to lead a single life rather than being in a toxic relationship.

Ok I get that, no one likes to be in a toxic relationship but what some people don’t like the most is being single so they choose the first. They just aren’t aware of how detrimental that is to their mental health, for me it is better to remain single for a period of time while focusing on some other goals than being in a toxic relationship or settle for less.

When someone shows who they are with actions, believe them.

2 - The second is around the concept of True Colors, what is that? This is simply the true nature or character of someone in a relationship.

“When someone shows who they are with actions, not words, believe them. Words mean nothing, actions mean everything”. a quora user wrote.
This is something I myself struggled with in the past, I would always try to see the best side in people even when they clearly show the opposite, I would just ignore their colors.
These are some lessons I hope you apply as you go through life and your relationships.

If you’ve read through to the end, congrats. I know this one has been long

Have a beautiful day and see you around!
