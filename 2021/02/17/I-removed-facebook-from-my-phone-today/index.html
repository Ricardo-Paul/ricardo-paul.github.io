I removed facebook from my phone today.

Ricardo Paul, written on
2021-02-17
distraction, focus

Today marks the beginning of a series of deep work sessions for me, I removed facebook from my phone for at least a month. Let me explain why.

More often than not, I find facebook to be a needlessly addictive app
If you use facebook heavily in your day to day, it is fair to say that you cannot imagine yourself doing without it for a long period of time, for many people, facebook is part of the social media they check first thing in the morning, including myself I should say. So we all know how addictive that is but fewer of us know the complications that it entails.
I am a life long learner
My hunger to learn new stuff and my willingness to improve myself never stops. Many times, I use the time spent on my phone scrolling through my news feed to learn stuff on the go, I should say things that are not related to a particular field, even though I try my best to personalize my news feed around nearly one thing.

But here's the truth:

You don't learn deeply and you cannot learn complicated things just scrolling down your feed, there's just too much content to go over on facebook and they're all unrelated. So that's maybe a good way to brush up on your existing knowledge but a wrong way to pick up new ones. 

Besides that, to me facebook is all about distraction, and how do we learn deeply?
Obviously is by staying focused.
Basically for me is through “long unbroken period of time” without quick-checking any source of distraction.

So I decided today to start honing in on some skills and acquire new ones.

What I'll do instead of facebooking
So what I do now instead of facebooking when I am off my computer? Well, whenever I wander through the apps on my phone and can't find facebook. I end up opening:

Quora: a question-answer based app so I can read some beautiful reflections.
Podcasts: The google podcast app so I can listen to and learn from amazing content that interests me.
Or my note taking apps: So I can read up on my previous notes and write new ones.

I kiss goodbye to the world of distractions !
I say hello to the world of deep focus, productivity and effectiveness!
